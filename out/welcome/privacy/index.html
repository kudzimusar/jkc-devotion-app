Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our website, including when you submit our contact form, sign up for Church OS, or make a giving inquiry. This may include your name, email address, and any message content you choose to share.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, send you updates about Japan Kingdom Church services and events, and improve our website and services. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

Giving and Financial Data

Financial transactions made through Tithe.ly, PayPal, Cash App, or Zelle are processed directly by those third-party platforms under their respective privacy policies. We do not store credit card or banking details on our servers.

Church OS Member Data

If you create a Church OS account, your devotional progress, attendance records, and journal entries are stored securely in our database hosted by Supabase. This data is used solely to support your spiritual growth and is only accessible to you and authorized church leadership.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and user session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Contact Us
