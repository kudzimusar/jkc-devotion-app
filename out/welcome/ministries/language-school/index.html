Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to speak any Japanese to join?

A: No. The Beginners track starts from zero.

Q: What if I miss a class?

A: Materials are uploaded to the student portal after each session.

Q: Can I switch tracks?

A: Yes, contact us within the first two sessions.

Q: Is there a refund policy?

A: Fees are non-refundable after the second class. Contact us if you have special circumstances.

Q: Where is the class held?

A: TE Akishima Building 3F, 2-1-6 Showa-cho, Akishima City, Tokyo 196-0015. 2-minute walk from JR Akishima Station.
