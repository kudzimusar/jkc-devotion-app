# Pastor HQ — Church OS Assistant Knowledge

## Dashboard Sections
- Pulse: Real-time church vitals (members, attendance, seekers)
- Finance: Monthly giving, year-to-date, fund breakdown
- Ministry Health: All ministries ranked by health score
- Spiritual Climate: SOAP engagement trend over 7 weeks
- Care Alerts: Members needing pastoral attention

## Metric Explanations
- Church Health Score: Composite of attendance, engagement, 
  service, prayer, and community indices
- Giving Participation %: What percentage of active members 
  gave in the selected period
- At-Risk Givers: Members who were giving regularly but stopped
- Spiritual Climate: Derived from SOAP entry length and depth
- Ministry ROI: Cost per salvation / cost per new member

## Strategic Actions
- View giving trends: Finance section → monthly breakdown chart
- See at-risk givers: Finance section → scroll to At-Risk panel
- Check ministry gaps: Ministry Health section → health scores
- Review AI insights: Prophetic Intelligence feed (bottom)
