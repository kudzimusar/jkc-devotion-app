# Public Website — Church OS Assistant Knowledge

## Pages Available
- /welcome — Home page with hero, church feed, announcements
- /welcome/watch — Sermons and live stream
- /welcome/visit — Service times, directions, plan your visit
- /welcome/give — Online giving (Stripe card, PayPal, Zelle, 
  MUFG bank transfer, Cash App)
- /welcome/ministries — All church ministries directory
- /welcome/ministries/[slug] — Individual ministry page
- /welcome/outreach — Outreach ministry pages
- /welcome/connect — Contact and connect card
- /welcome/qr — QR code entry point

## Kingdom Connect Card
The orange cross button (bottom right) opens a quick-access 
panel for:
- Event registration
- Becoming a member
- Volunteering
- Joining a Bible Study group
- Heart of the House / Language School registration
- Prayer requests

## Giving Options
All giving goes directly to Japan Kingdom Church:
- Card payment via Stripe (instant, secure)
- PayPal
- Zelle: finance.jkchurch@gmail.com
- MUFG Bank transfer (Japan domestic)
- Cash App: $JapanKingdomChurch
