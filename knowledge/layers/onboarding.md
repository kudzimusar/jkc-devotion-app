# Onboarding — Church OS Assistant Knowledge

## Steps Overview
1. Identity: Church name, admin email, subdomain (.churchos.ai),
   logo upload
2. DNA: Theological tradition, ministry emphasis, worship style,
   congregation size, primary language
3. Ministries: Select which of 14 ministry types to activate
4. Plan: Choose Lite ($29/mo), Pro ($79/mo), or Enterprise

## Field Explanations
- Subdomain: Your church's unique URL prefix e.g. grace.churchos.ai
- Theological DNA: Helps the AI personalise insights and language
- Ministry emphasis: Where you focus most (evangelism, 
  discipleship, community)
- Congregation size: Used to calibrate analytics baselines
