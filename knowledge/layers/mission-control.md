# Mission Control — Church OS Assistant Knowledge

## Dashboard Tabs
- Overview: Church health index, key stats at a glance
- Members: Full member directory, search, filter by status
- Attendance: Weekly attendance records and trends
- Ministry Hub: All 14 ministries, health scores, reports
- Pastoral Care: Member alerts, prayer requests, follow-ups
- Communications: Send messages, manage campaigns
- Finance: Giving totals, transactions, manual log entry
- Intelligence: AI-generated prophetic insights
- Website Inquiries: Connect card submissions from public site

## Key Metrics Explained
- Health Index: Weighted score (Attendance 40%, SOAP 40%, 
  Prayer 20%). Score of 47 is current JKC baseline.
- Member Alerts: Auto-generated when members miss services 
  or show disengagement signals
- Prophetic Insights: AI analysis of patterns across all data

## Common Actions
- Add member: Members tab → + New Member button
- Send message: Communications tab → New Campaign
- Log attendance: Attendance tab → + Record
- Respond to inquiry: Website Inquiries tab → click inquiry
