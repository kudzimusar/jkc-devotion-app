# 🕊️ Church OS: The Comprehensive Organization Manual

Welcome to the **Church OS** ecosystem. This manual is designed for church leaders, staff, and administrators to effectively operate the digital spiritual infrastructure. 

---

## 🚀 1. Accessing the Platform

### Console Login (Staff of Church OS)
The **Console** is the command center for the entire platform. If you are a staff member of the "Church OS" parent organization, this is where you manage multiple churches (tenants).

*   **URL**: `https://churchos.ai/login` (or locally at `http://localhost:3000/login`)
*   **Credentials**: Use your staff email (e.g., `admin@churchos.ai`). 
*   **Redirect**: After a successful login, you will be automatically redirected to the **Pastor HQ** or **Super Admin Dashboard**.

> [!TIP]
> **Staff Tip**: If you don't have a password yet, ensure your administrator has added you to the `admin_roles` table in the database with the `super_admin` role.

## 🛠️ 2. Core Modules & Testing Guide

### A. The Onboarding (New Organization Setup)
This is the "First Impression" for any new church joining the ecosystem.

**Step-by-Step Test:**
1.  **Identity**: Go to `/onboarding`. Enter the **Church Name** (e.g., "Grace Fellowship"), a **Contact Email**, and choose a **Subdomain** (e.g., `grace`). Upload a logo (512x512px).
2.  **DNA**: On the next screen, define the "Spiritual Core." Choose the tradition (e.g., *Pentecostal*), style (e.g., *Contemporary*), and primary language (e.g., *Japanese*).
3.  **Plan**: Select a tier: 
    *   **Lite**: Basic features.
    *   **Pro**: Full analytics + AI Prophetic insights.
    *   **Enterprise**: Custom branding.
4.  **Confirm**: Click "Start My Growth Journey." 
5.  **Verification**: You should see a "Sanctuary Provisioned Successfully" message and be redirected to your new dashboard.

---

### B. Mission Control (Leadership Dashboard)
The high-level flight deck for the Senior Pastor.

**What to Test:**
*   **Church Health Score**: Check the 0–100 gauge. Is it representing current activity?
*   **Engagement Grid**: Are you seeing real-time counts of total members and active devoters?
*   **AI Alerts**: Look for "Prophetic Insights" (e.g., "3 members have stopped journaling"). These are generated by analyzing congregation trends.
*   **Care Queue**: Verify that urgent prayer requests are appearing in the list for pastoral follow-up.

---

### C. Member Profile (The Connection Card)
Every member's digital identity. This feeds the "Intelligence Layer" of the church.

**Step-by-Step Test:**
1.  **Access**: Log in as a member and go to `/profile`.
2.  **Identity**: Update your "Spiritual Journey" (e.g., Salvation Date, Baptism Status).
3.  **Skills**: Add your professional skills (e.g., "Graphic Design," "Youth Counseling"). 
4.  **Household**: Link your family members to create a "Household Unit."
5.  **Save**: Ensure all changes persist when you refresh the page.

---

### D. Public Website & Devotion App
The "Daily Bread" for members.

**What to Test:**
*   **Daily Scripture**: Does the passage match the current date?
*   **Bilingual Toggle**: Click the EN/JP button. Does the text switch instantly?
*   **SOAP Journal**: Type an entry using the **S**cripture, **O**bservation, **A**pplication, **P**rayer method. 
*   **Bible AI**: Ask a question like "Who is Jesus?" based on the day's text. Does the AI respond in a "Spirit-led" tone?
*   **Sunday Check-In**: On a Sunday, verify the "I'm Here" button appears.

## 📈 3. Strategic AI Shepherd
The "Secret Sauce" of Church OS. 

The **AI Shepherd** aggregates anonymized journal entries to find global trends. 
*   **Example**: If 50% of the church is journaling about "Peace," the AI highlights this to the Pastor.
*   **Testing Clue**: Check the "Community Mood" section in Mission Control to see if it's picking up on member sentiment.

---

## 💡 Tips for Non-Technical Users (Pastors)

*   **"What is a Subdomain?"**: This is just your church's name in the web address (e.g., `grace.churchos.ai`). It’s like your digital street address.
*   **"Is the Journal Private?"**: Yes! Pastors only see *trends* (e.g., "The church is feeling anxious") and never your specific personal entries unless you share them.
*   **"The AI Alerts"**: These are NOT instructions, they are "clues" to help you shepherd the flock better. Use them as conversation starters.
